About Us

MSU Prov Chapter

The objectives of our chapter are to uphold the ideals and standards of The Delta Chi Fraternity and to build a strong chapter where membership is an honor and privilege.

We foster genuine brotherhood, develop leadership, and aim to “make good men great and great men excellent.”
